== Description ==

Include a widget of recent from a remind101 account.


== Installation ==

1. upload to your `wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in wordpress
3. Move the widget where you want in in the Appearance -> Widgets Menu
4. Input your token from [remind101.com](http://www.remind101.com). To find your token, login to your account at remind101.com, click on Account then My Widget. Your key is the string of letters and numbers after token= up to but not including the quotation marks.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I find my remind101 token =
Login to your account at [remind101.com](http://www.remind101.com), click on Account then My Widget. Your key is the string of letters and numbers after token= up to but not including the quotation marks.
